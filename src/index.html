Fr. Tien Cao

Parish Priest, Catholic Parish of Mackenzie

Nau mai, haere mai, welcome to the Catholic Parish of Mackenzie, located in the central South Island of Aotearoa, New Zealand. Our parish encompasses a diverse geographical area, stretching across several beautiful towns and villages. With churches in Fairlie, Tekapo, and Twizel, we are deeply rooted in the heart of this captivating region. Our parish proudly serves the local communities, offering spiritual guidance, support, and a welcoming space for all who seek solace, fellowship, and a deeper connection with their faith.
